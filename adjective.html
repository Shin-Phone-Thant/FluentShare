What are Adjectives?

An adjective is a word that describes or modifies a noun or pronoun, providing more information about its qualities, quantity, or state. Adjectives help to make sentences more specific and descriptive.

Examples:

Qualities The blue sky.
The white clouds.
Quantity She has four dogs.
There is little food left.
State The classroom is empty.
He looks sad.
Characeteristics of Adjectives What kind? (e.g., a small house)
Which one? (e.g., that black coat)
How many? (e.g., four bananas)
How much? (e.g., some money)
